Welcome to the RMF Analysis Tool

RMF (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) analysis is a customer segmentation technique used in marketing that groups customers based on their purchasing behavior.

  • Recency (R): How recently a customer made a purchase
  • Frequency (F): How often a customer makes purchases
  • Monetary (M): How much money a customer spends

This tool allows you to upload your customer data and perform RMF analysis to identify valuable customer segments.
